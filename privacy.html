Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 18, 2026 (updated for accuracy)

Summary

PinTop does not collect analytics, telemetry, or advertising data. Window capture stays on your Mac. The direct (Gumroad) edition makes one optional network request when you activate a license key.

Screen Recording

PinTop uses macOS Screen Recording permission to capture window content for the always-on-top overlay. This capture is:

  • Processed on your device in real time
  • Never saved to disk as a recording
  • Never uploaded for analytics or cloud sync
  • Discarded after each frame is shown

Accessibility

PinTop uses macOS Accessibility permission to:

  • Register global keyboard shortcuts
  • Move and resize windows for snap and zoom

No window content, text, or keystrokes are logged through Accessibility.

What we do not do

  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No usage tracking dashboards
  • No crash reports sent automatically
  • No ads or third-party trackers

License verification (Gumroad / direct edition)

When you paste a Gumroad license key and activate, PinTop may send a one-time HTTPS request to Gumroad’s license API (api.gumroad.com) with the license key (and product identity). That check confirms the key is valid and not refunded.

  • No Screen Recording frames are sent
  • Hardware binding for the trial and key is stored in your Mac’s Keychain
  • After a successful activation, day-to-day use does not require ongoing network access

If Gumroad is unreachable, offline / support keys may still work when issued that way.

Local storage

Preferences (e.g. border colour, opacity, Mini PiP size, activity highlight) and trial/license state are stored locally via macOS UserDefaults and Keychain.

Children’s privacy

PinTop is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect children’s data.

Changes

Updates to this policy will be posted here with a new effective date.
