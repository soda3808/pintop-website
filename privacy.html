Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 6, 2026

Summary

PinTop does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your Mac.

Screen Recording

PinTop uses macOS Screen Recording permission to capture window content for the always-on-top overlay display. This capture is:

  • Processed entirely on your device in real-time
  • Never saved to disk
  • Never transmitted over any network
  • Immediately discarded after rendering each frame

Accessibility

PinTop uses macOS Accessibility permission to:

  • Register global keyboard shortcuts
  • Move and resize windows for snap and zoom features

No window content, text, or user input is read or recorded through the Accessibility API.

Data Collection

PinTop does not:

  • Collect any analytics or telemetry
  • Track usage patterns
  • Send crash reports automatically
  • Connect to any server or external service
  • Store any user data, preferences, or settings externally

Third-Party Services

PinTop does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Local Storage

PinTop stores minimal preferences (such as launch-at-login setting) using macOS system APIs. These preferences remain on your device and are managed by macOS.

License Verification (Direct Distribution Only)

For the version distributed outside the Mac App Store, PinTop generates a hardware identifier derived from your Mac's hardware properties. This identifier is:

  • Used solely to bind your license key to your specific Mac
  • Computed locally and never automatically transmitted to any server
  • A one-way hash that cannot be reversed to reveal hardware details
  • Displayed in the license activation dialog so you may voluntarily share it with us when requesting a license key

The Mac App Store version does not use hardware identifiers — Apple handles all license validation.

Children's Privacy

PinTop does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
